## Hot-spots for a paid B2C sports-prediction product

### 1. AI disclosure — REQUIRED

From the AUP:

> "If model outputs are presented directly to individuals or consumers, you
> must disclose to them that you are using AI to help produce your advice,
> decisions, or recommendations."

**MatchForecast status:** ✅ done.

- Match detail footer: `Baseline v1 · AI prediction by Claude`
- Global app footer: `Predictions by Claude (Anthropic).`
- Match-detail disclaimer block names this as a model prediction explicitly

### 2. "May be false / misleading" warning — REQUIRED

Commercial Terms §D.3 (paraphrased from fetch):

> "Customer acknowledges, and must notify its Users, that factual assertions
> in Outputs should not be relied upon without independently checking their
> accuracy, as they may be false, incomplete, misleading or not reflective
> of recent events or information."

**MatchForecast status:** ⚠️ partially. We say "for entertainment, not
betting advice" but we don't currently say "may be false, incomplete or
misleading — verify independently." We need this in our own ToS page and
ideally on the match-detail disclaimer block.

### 3. Financial-decision use case

AUP carve-out:

> "Use cases related to financial decisions, including investment advice,
> loan approvals, and determining financial eligibility or creditworthiness"
> require human-in-the-loop review and disclosure before dissemination to
> consumers.

**MatchForecast status:** does not apply. We do not provide financial advice
and we explicitly frame the output as entertainment, not betting advice
(design §8). Keep the framing crisp in ToS so we don't drift into the
financial-decision bucket.

### 4. Sports betting / gambling

No explicit clause found in either document.

**MatchForecast status:** We sit on the safer side because the "entertainment,
not betting advice" disclaimer is in the global footer + match-detail
disclaimer. Do not show odds, do not recommend bets, do not link to bookies.

### 5. Minors

AUP:

> "Products serving minors must comply with the additional guidelines"
> outlined in the Help Center.

**MatchForecast status:** ⚠️ not addressed yet. Add an "18+" or "13+"
acknowledgement in our ToS and Privacy Policy at signup (Clerk supports
custom signup fields). Decision pending — see launch-prep checklist.

### 6. Ownership of outputs — ✅ ours

Commercial Terms §B:

> "Anthropic hereby assigns to Customer its right, title and interest (if any)
> in and to Outputs."

**MatchForecast status:** the predictions are ours; we may store, modify,
display, and sell access to them.

### 7. Liability cap (Anthropic-side)

Commercial Terms §L.3.a (paraphrased):

> "Liability of each party is limited to Fees paid by Customer in the
> previous 12 months" and excludes consequential / incidental / special /
> indirect damages.

§L.2:

> "ANTHROPIC DOES NOT WARRANT that the Services or Outputs are accurate,
> complete or error-free."

**MatchForecast status:** worth mirroring this in our own ToS — our liability
to users is capped at the price they paid (or zero for free tier).
